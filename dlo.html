Empowering the Token Holder Community

With the XFai DLO, any partnering tokens are able to participate in seamless liquidity mining. Powering the DEX Liquidity

XFai DLO charts the orderbook and liquidity across multiple centralized exchanges, and reflects the synthetic curve in a decentralized exchange. The XFai DLO adds and removes liquidity to provide the optimal trading ecosystem on DEX for the traders of the partnered tokens, with less slippage, supporting the continuous growth of the DLO partner.

Automated Passive Income for Treasury

DLO partners are eligible to stake their treasury funds in the XFai DLO, earning APY while providing liquidity for their token holders. Not only does it benefit the token traders by preventing huge slippage that often occurs on centralized exchanges with automated market makers, but it also signals to the community that the project itself believes in the future of the token.

Zero Cost Integration

XFai DLO is completely free to use. DLO partners do not have to provide any tokens. Once the partnership has been reached, the DLO partner pool will be created in the XFai DLO platform, inviting liquidity miners to purchase the partnered token for liquidity farming while encouraging the token holders to choose a new way of realizing profit without having to sell the tokens. The integration is seamless, efficient, and costless..
